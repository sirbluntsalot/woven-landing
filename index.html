The rabbit hole engine

Audiences drift.
Rabbit holes pull. Trace one.

Nobody jumps from gym content to hyper-political punditry in one leap. The algorithm walks them there, one recommendation at a time. Woven maps that drift as a weighted graph over the entire creator space and runs real pathfinding on it: the likeliest routes your persuadable voters are already traveling, and the exact hop where a message can still reach them.

  • Audience flow becomes a weighted graph: shortest-path routing surfaces the likeliest journeys, not anecdotes
  • Every route ends at an attractor: the ecosystem pulling hardest on that audience
  • The intercept window is hop one: book the gateway voice before the slide finishes
